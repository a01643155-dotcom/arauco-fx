Executive Summary

Why does currency risk matter for Arauco?

Arauco operates in 11 countries with revenues in USD, EUR and multiple local currencies, while its Mexico costs are denominated in MXN. This mismatch is at the core of its FX exposure.

Global Presence

Operations in Chile, Brazil, Uruguay, Argentina, Peru, Colombia, USA, Canada, Mexico and Europe. 37% of revenues from North America.

11 countriesUSD / EUR / MXN

Mexico Market Leader

#1 panel board manufacturer in Mexico, USA, Canada and LATAM. Plants in Durango, Zitácuaro (Michoacán) and Cuautitlán (State of Mexico).

MDFPBO/MDPMelamine

USD/MXN Exposure

Key raw materials — urea, methanol, phenol — priced in USD. Local revenues in MXN. MXN depreciation directly raises operating costs.

Urea USDMethanol USDCOGS in USD

Hedging Strategy

Arauco manages its exposure through natural hedging and local currency financing. No financial derivatives currently used in Mexico.
